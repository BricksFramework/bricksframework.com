Bricks Framework

A modern framework to keep your projects awesome and light

Create awesome projects with web technologies you love.

An open source front-end and back-end framework to develop with HTML5.

  • What is Bricks?


    A framework built with bricks. Just use the addons (Bricks) you need to reach your new project.

  • Who is it for?


    Built for developers, designers and crazy people with new ideas. Great to start small and big projects.

  • Why you'll love it?


    Most of the libraries are built with more components and functions that you don't need. Bricks only have what you will use and no more.

    Use what you need


    Do not load more than you need on your project. Build a custom library with only the components that are important and usefull for you.

    Fully responsive


    Spend less time managing screen sizes and more time on building a perfect design. Your project will look amazing across all the modern devices and browsers

    Extend the power


    Bricks is light and sometimes you need more things to improve your project. You can extend Bricks functionality and design with awesome addons.

    Built by people like you


    Built and maintained by developers passionated about web technologies and web standars. Open sourced and managed through Github.
